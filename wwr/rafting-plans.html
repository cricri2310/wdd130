Beginner River Adventure

Perfect for families and beginners, this half-day rafting experience provides a gentle introduction to river rafting with plenty of scenic views and manageable rapids. Our certified guides will teach basic paddling techniques, safety tips, and provide all equipment for a thrilling yet safe ride on the river.

Thrill Seeker's Challenge

This full-day rafting trip is ideal for adventure enthusiasts looking for a thrilling experience with more challenging rapids and longer time on the water. Tackle Class III-IV rapids while surrounded by stunning natural scenery. Our expert guides will lead you safely through each rapid while ensuring you have the time of your life.
